Clean Water, Healthy Lives Initiative

Project Overview

This project provides clean and safe drinking water to the community of Biankouri, Togo, improving health and overall well-being. Residents previously relied on unsafe water sources, leading to waterborne diseases.

Key Objectives:

  • Install sustainable water supply systems (boreholes/filtration units)
  • Reduce waterborne diseases and improve hygiene practices
  • Educate the community on water conservation and maintenance

Expected Impact:

  • Better health outcomes with fewer water-related illnesses
  • Reduced time spent fetching water (especially benefiting children's education)
  • Economic and social benefits for women and families
