Supporting Amazing Shepherd Orphanage

Caring for Vulnerable Children

This initiative provides essential support to Amazing Shepherd Orphanage in Ghana, ensuring children receive proper care, education, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Orphanage Support Highlights

Essential Supplies

Monthly provision of food, clothing, and hygiene products for 45 resident children

Educational Support

School fees, uniforms, and learning materials for all school-age children

Facility Improvements

Renovation of dormitories and installation of water purification system

Impact on the Children

Our partnership with Amazing Shepherd Orphanage has significantly improved quality of life for the children:

  • 100% school enrollment for eligible children
  • Nutritional meals provided daily
  • Safe and clean living environment
  • Psychosocial support through counseling services

Ongoing Support
