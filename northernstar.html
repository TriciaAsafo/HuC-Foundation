Project Northern Star

Improving Educational Conditions in Wa

This initiative focuses on enhancing the learning environment at Northern Star Senior High School in Wa, Ghana, through infrastructure improvements and resource provision.

Key Objectives:

  • Construct a water reservoir system to provide reliable water access
  • Renovate and equip classroom facilities
  • Provide modern educational materials
  • Improve sanitation facilities with water-dependent systems
  • Establish sustainable water solutions for the school community
  • Create a mentorship program for students
