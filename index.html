Empowering Communities for a Brighter Future

HearturCommunity Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the total development of people in underprivileged communities.

Formally registered as a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) under the codes of Ghana in May 2022, with the express purpose of collaborating with other partners and sponsors to improve communities' access to basic needs.

Since 2014, we've worked to close the gap in livelihoods of people in very deprived communities in Ghana that lack access to basic needs such as schools, hospitals, clean water, libraries, and vocational centers.
