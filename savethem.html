Christmas 2016 at Save Them Young Orphanage

Bringing Holiday Joy to Children

In December 2016, HearturCommunity Foundation visited Save Them Young Orphanage in Ashaiman to celebrate Christmas with the children and deliver essential donations.

Donation Highlights

Christmas Gifts

Toys, clothes, and personal items for each child

Food Supplies

Special holiday meals and 3 months of groceries

Educational Materials

Books, school supplies, and learning aids

Christmas Celebration

Our team spent the day with the children, creating special memories through:

  • Christmas carols and performances
  • Gift-giving ceremony
  • Special holiday meal together
  • Games and activities
  • Photo session with Santa

Lasting Impact

This special Christmas initiative:

  • Brought holiday cheer to 32 orphaned children
  • Provided essential supplies for the new year
  • Created cherished memories for the children
  • Marked the beginning of our ongoing relationship with the orphanage
